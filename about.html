About the Project

Overview

The DOJ Legal Assistance Chatbot is an AI-based web application designed to provide instant and reliable responses to commonly asked legal and judicial queries. The system aims to improve accessibility to legal information through a simple conversational interface.

Problem Statement

Many users face difficulties in understanding legal procedures due to complex terminology, lack of guidance, and limited access to official legal resources. Existing platforms often require extensive manual navigation or prior legal knowledge.

Proposed Solution

This project proposes an AI-powered chatbot that interacts with users in natural language and provides structured legal information related to courts, FIRs, rights, and judicial processes. The system is designed to act as an informational assistant, not as a substitute for legal advice.
