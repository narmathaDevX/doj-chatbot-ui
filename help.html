Help & FAQ

How to Use the Chatbot

Navigate to the Chatbot page and type your legal or judicial question in the input box. The chatbot will respond with relevant information based on your query.

What Kind of Questions Can I Ask?

You may ask questions related to general legal procedures such as:

  • What is an FIR?
  • Basic court procedures
  • Legal rights and duties
  • Judicial terminology

Is This Legal Advice?

No. This chatbot is intended only for informational purposes. It does not provide legal advice and should not be considered a replacement for consultation with legal professionals.

Data & Privacy

User queries are processed only to generate responses. No personal data is stored or shared.
